Standardized Wheelchair for Ride Integration

2019-2020

Proposal
We designed and prototyped a wheelchair so that wheelchair riders would be able to go onto rides in 15 seconds or less. This allowed wheelchair riders to more efficiently access rides at Universal Studios.

Subgroups
Controls, Locking, Restraints, Professional Research

Walking Wellness for Airlines

2018-2019

Proposal
We designed and implemented a collapsable treadmill integrated with the attendant seat apparatus at cross-aisles to achieve zero seat count loss. Benefits of this included passenger well-being and reduce risk for passengers prone to DVT.

Subgroups
User Interface, Storage and Deployment, Operation and Power, Professional Research

Awards
3rd at the SWE National Conference

Crew Overboard Alarm System

2017-2018

Proposal
We developed an alarm system that detects when a crew member is no longer on board a vessel. We implemented a visible and audible alarm system that also records the last location of the crew members, calculates the area using a direct rate algorithm, and displays all the info on an iPad.

Subgroups
Overboard Detection, Materials, Network and Communications, Location Tracking and Display

Awards
1st at the SWE National Conference

Solar-Powered Water Filtration System

2016-2017

Proposal
The Ebola crisis of 2014 decimated healthcare facilities in Sierra Leone because residents drank river water that was polluted with upstream waste and sewage disposal. We implemented a device that was entirely off of on-site solar power to clean, purify, and treat river water and be implemented in healthcare facilities.

Subgroups
Energy Generation, Unit Design, Water Treatment, Energy Storage

Awards
3rd at the SWE National Conference

Localization Device for a Catheter Tip

2015-2016

Proposal
We created a localization device for a catheter tip. This device would offer continuous monitoring of the location of the non-sensor enabled catheter rip while engaged in the coronary network.

Subgroups
Photocell, Rotating Box, Software, Trackball

Awards
2nd at the SWE National Conference
