Walking Wellness for Airlines

2018-2019

Proposal
We designed and implemented a collapsable treadmill integrated with the attendant seat apparatus at cross-aisles to achieve zero seat count loss. Benefits of this included passenger well-being and potentially reducing risk for passengers prone to DVT.

Subgroups
User Interface, Storage and Deployment, Operation and Power, Professional Research
