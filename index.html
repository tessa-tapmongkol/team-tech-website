Cal Poly Society of Women Engineers Team Tech

Team Tech is a multidisciplinary, competitive engineering design club apart of Cal Poly's Society of Women Engineers. Every year we partner with a different company to address a problem statement with an engineering solution. Our goal is to provide students with real world engineering experience that allow them to develop technical skills and make a social impact.
